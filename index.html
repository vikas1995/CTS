OUR
MISSION

Coates-Terry Scholarship Fund is a 501 (c) (3) nonprofit entity. Established in 2003, our purpose is to provide financial support for students pursuing college degrees. We focus our support on first-generation Black & other marginalized students pursing degrees.

OUR PRIMARY GOALS

Government grants & financial aid still leaves students with debts too great to remain in college. Students often leave College without degrees because of insufficient financial support.

Financial Support to Needy students
Reduce Drop-Out Rates Due to Finances
Improve Retention Rates, Graduation Rates & Degrees Earned

SCHOLARSHIP
REQUIREMENTS

  • Be a U.S. Citizen
  • Be currently enrolled in or accepted by an accredited USA College or University.
  • Be a full-time or part-time student.
  • Possess minimum grade point average (GPA) of 2.5 (C+) on a 4.0 system at time of application.
  • Must be current or have prior youth ministry experience
  • Must demonstrate financial need
  • Have no criminal record or activity
  • Current or prior member of First Baptist Church Merrifield
  • Seminary/Divinity School Students must possess a 3.0 GPA and all other above listed requirements.

APPLICATION
SCREENING PROCESS

  • Applications will be reviewed by the Advisory Board.
  • First priority given to current members of FBCM.
  • Applicants may be asked to appear to clarify packet information.
  • Decisions and Announcement of Awards will be decided within a week of deliberations.
  • Applicants must be approved by Director/Co-Director.
